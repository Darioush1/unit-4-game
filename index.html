Crystal Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on the crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game if your total is equal to the randomly generated number. You lose if your total goes over the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you.


Each time the game ends the value of the cry will change.
